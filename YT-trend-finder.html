Privacy Policy for YT Trend Finder

Effective Date: May 11, 2025

YT Trend Finder is a Chrome extension designed to help YouTubers discover trending content ideas using publicly available sources like Google Trends, Wikipedia, and AnswerThePublic.

What Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal or sensitive user data. All processing is done locally in the browser. We do not use cookies, analytics, or tracking tools of any kind.

Third-Party APIs

We may display publicly available data retrieved from websites like Google Trends or Wikipedia. These sites have their own privacy policies and terms of use.

Your Privacy

We respect your privacy. No data is logged, shared, or sold. The extension functions without any user login or data sync.

Thank you for using YT Trend Finder.
